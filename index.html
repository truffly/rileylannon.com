I am a composer from the Twin Cities, Minnesota, USA. I have over a decade of piano experience and 6 years experience producing music using Logic Pro, Ableton Live, and Native Instruments Komplete.

Riley Lannon

Musician, Composer, Producer

Production

Using industry-standard software such as Ableton Live and Native Instruments KOMPLETE, I produce, mix, and master all of my own compositions, giving me the freedom to produce a recording that sounds exactly as I intended

Composition

Described as "cinematic", my composition style is modern, influenced by a wide range of composers including J.S. Bach, Ludwig van Beethoven, Dmitri Shostakovich, Hans Zimmer, and more.

Performance

Classically trained, I have been playing keyboard instruments for over a decade, including piano, organ, and harpsichord.
